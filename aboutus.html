Freedom For You Foundation

A Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) that actively empowers the citizens, especially the under-privileged, by conducting Awareness, Education and Training programs with a focus on the areas of Socio-Economic, Health & Wellness, Emergency Medical Response & Disaster Risk Mitigation, Disaster Relief & Rehabilitation, WASH (Water & Sanitation Hygiene), and various other areas since the last 14 years!

About Us

Our Mission

EMPOWER, EDUACTE & ENHANCE lives at the bottom of the pyramid, and instill in them a sense of Socio-Economic Freedom.

Freedom4U conducts programs in 3 spheres viz., Awareness, Education & Training in areas such as Health & Wellness, Safety & Security, Financial Inclusion, Socio-Environomic empowerment programs with a focus on Sustainability, Inclusiveness, Eco-friendly, with a Mission to 'SAVE LIFE AROUND YOU'.

Our Practices

Freedom4U has been registered as a not-for-profit charitable trust with the Charity Commissioner, Maharashtra, under Bombay Oublic Trusts Act, 1950. We have partnered with many leading MNCs/Govt./Social Organizations like Dow Chemical, Corteva (formerly Dow AgroSciences), Huntsman, Nuclear Power Corporation, Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, Blue Star, SBI Life Insurance, Micro-Technologies, TCS,>Sandoz, Rotary Clubs, Raptim Research, etc. for implementing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects including Capacity Building training programs for the benefit of the local population, and ERT training for their executives/staff/workers.

Freedom4U also provides consulting to a few MSME companies for designing their CSR policies as per the Companies Act; hand-holding from the stage of ideating to implementing/monitoring/evaluation of the CSR projects, and also suggesting CSR activities from its repertory of over 1000 CSR project ideas, to fit the CSR budgets.

Management

Freedom4U has been set-up and managed by professionals from various walks of life, who have worked with a mission of 'Selfless Service'. It has a 3 member Board of Trustees, and the Executive Committee comprises of senior professionals from the top-management in private/govt. sector, Doctors, Engineers, Social Workers, a Chartered Accountant, and a legal professional. All of these motivated individuals contribute their might on an honorary status.

We have over 36 resource persons, 12 staff members, 40 empanelled doctors, 79 volunteers to support it's activites/projects in focus areas viz., Education, Health, WASH, Livelihood Training, Relief & Rehab, Safety & Security, Empowerment of girls/women/physically challenged individuals, etc.

During the FY2014-15, CEO & Managing Truste, Dr. Ravi S Kumar, was absorbed as a full time consultant, by Ministry of Home Affairs, Govt. of India, New Delhi, designated as a Project Director in the office of the Directorate General of Fire Services, Civil Defence & Home Guards for monitoring, implementing and training, over 11 million citizens, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Mainstreaming Civil Defence in Disaster Risk Reduction throughout the country at an outlay of INR 291 Cr.
