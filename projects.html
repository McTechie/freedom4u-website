Woman Empowerment  

Challenge

Poor women, single mothers, widows & divorcees don't have assured sources of earning & livelihood. Most are overworked, under-paid, maid servants earning 10$ monthly from each home. With no work in the afternoon, few work in civil construction sites earning a pittance of what a man would earn. Migratory laborers, especially women, are fleeced, ill-treated and subject themselves to 10 hours of manual work a day due to fear of losing their job. Some are also forced into the flesh trade.

Solution

100 women in groups of 10 will be taught design & manufacturing skills to weave jute bags, home linen and ladies dresses. One set of equipment will also be given to each group. They will be trained in entrepreneurial, business management and marketing skills, and will have an assured regular income source for a better living. Apart from this, ill-treated women will earn respect from their relatives and will have enhanced confidence & socio-economic improvement.

Disaster Response  

Challenge

This Program will train 50 youth to become 'First-Responder Youth Brigade' in each node of Navi Mumbai starting with Airoli node. Navi Mumbai-Uran-Thane belt is in an earthquake zone-3 area; besides it is densely populated by people & industries that may result in man-made disasters too. Untrained bystander & rescuer is unable or unwilling to help hapless victims of any disaster leading to preventable death. Many women/children are main casualties in most disasters, calling for immediate ERT programs.

Solution

50 Youth from each node of Navi Mumbai would be trained to learn ABCs of First-Aid, Effective CPR, & Emergency Response techniques & be a part of First-Responder 'Youth Brigade' in that node. 'Youth Brigade' shall attend to casualties during the Golden Minutes of an incident. Women & children are major casualties in any disaster; women would be trained in ERT that would help them save their kith/kin too.
