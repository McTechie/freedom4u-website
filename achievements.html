Freedom For You Foundation

A Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) that actively empowers the citizens, especially the under-privileged, by conducting Awareness, Education and Training programs with a focus on the areas of Socio-Economic, Health & Wellness, Emergency Medical Response & Disaster Risk Mitigation, Disaster Relief & Rehabilitation, WASH (Water & Sanitation Hygiene), and various other areas since the last 14 years!

Our Feats & Achievements

  • Super Star in Global Giving UK (GGUK)

    Freedom4U has been upgraded to Super Star under Global Giving UK's Reward Point status; GGUK has funded us to the extent of approx 3 lakhs for our woman empowerment program using sustainable livelihood training to make these marginalized women, an entrepreneur in their own rights using bag and dress making silks.

  • Charity Aid Foundation America (CAFA)

    Freedom4U has qualified under CAFA norms for receiving donations from US tax payers, both individuals and corporates, and in turn these tax payers can make 100% tax efficient donations to get deductions to that extent from their income tax liability.

  • Capacity Building

    A record number of 22500 people till date were trained by us as a First Responder under our Mission "Save Life Around You". A part of the equipment used in the mission was donated by Dow Chemical - Kalwa plant in 2010

  • SAFE SCHOOL Mission

    Many schools/colleges were trained in Disaster Management/Fire Drill/First-Responder training, under our unique "Safe School Mission". A part of the equipment used in the mission was donated by Dow Chemical - Kalwa plant.

  • SAFE SOCIETY Mission

    MMR region being in Earthquake Zone-4, it is imperative that the citizens know the risk to life/property. Freedom4U's "Safe Society Mission" sensitizes the citizens in Coop. Hsg. Societies, in Emergency Medical Response, Disaster Preparedness, Emergency Rescue Techniques, etc. Our programs have impacted over 108 societies in the last 8 years.

  • Participation in All India Exhibition

    We were invited to participate and exhibit our service initiatives for community enrichment in "Give India 2014- a NGO- Corporate Conclave cum Exhibition" in Sept 2014, largest of its kind annual conference, held at Worli - Mumbai

  • Started a Play School

    We started a play school in December 2014 for children of age 2-5 years, of daily wage earners in Rabale slums. The play school witnessed 73 children for the academic year in April 2016, and as on date there are 40 students upto the age of 5 years.

  • Set up a Disaster Relief Fund

    In February 2015, we set-up a separate Disaster Relief Fund for providing emergency relief materials to hapless victims of disasters. We helped Nepal/Bihar Earthquake victims in April/May 2015, Chennai floods in December 2015; Donations to this fund is Tax Exempt.

  • Set up an Education Assistance Fund

    In June 2014, we set-up a separate Education Assistance Fund to help poor but studious children to pursue higher education. We pay their Engg. College/Jr. College fees/clothing/books etc. Over 10 students have benefitted till date including one who is in SE (Second Year of Engineering) in Civil Engineering in VJTI, Matunga - Mumbai

  • Vitamin A / Dewroming Supplementation (VASD)

    Freedom4U was selected by US based Vitamin Angels for administering Vitamin A to babies (6 months - 59 months) & De-worming tablets (12 months - 59 months children), once in 6 months until having attained 5 years of age. Over 5000 children have benefitted.

  • New Clientele / Partners-in-Service

    We added many elite Corporates/Istitutions/NGOs to our portfolio during this year

  • Sustainable Skill Training Centres

    We successfully set-up 7 training centres in Navi Mumbai, Taloja and Khed Talukas for empowering marginalized girls/women from semi-urban slums and rural areas.
