Freedom For You Foundation

A Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) that actively empowers the citizens, especially the under-privileged, by conducting Awareness, Education and Training programs with a focus on the areas of Socio-Economic, Health & Wellness, Emergency Medical Response & Disaster Risk Mitigation, Disaster Relief & Rehabilitation, WASH (Water & Sanitation Hygiene), and various other areas since the last 14 years!

Make a Donation!
Training 100 Poor Women in Entrepreneurship

Training groups of 10 poor single mothers, widows, divorcees, school & college dropouts in different nodes of Navi Mumbai in designing jute woven bags, home linen, ladies dresses and the manufacturing process of paper artifacts.

First-Responder Youth Brigade

Forming a First-Responder Youth Brigade in different nodes of Navi Mumbai to help in any disaster calling for emergency response from trained First Responders, including attending to sudden cardiac arrest victims in their neighborhood.
